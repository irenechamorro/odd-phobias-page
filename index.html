ODD PHOBIAS

This website was made to display a series of experiemental AR filters made with Snapchat's

Lens Studio. The theme of strange phobias is a parody of mobile apps that use augmenting

exposure therapy for serious fears. Instead, these AR experiences show humorous phobias such

as Ephebiphobia, or fear of adolescents, to practice creating AR experiences.

PLUTOPHOBIA

fear of money

EPHEBIPHOBIA

fear of youth

XANTHOPHOBIA

fear of yellow

NOMOPHOBIA

fear of no phone

TAPHOPHOBIA

fear of being burried alive

To display the AR filters on the webpage, I added clips of each experience on a interactive

phone image. Since the actual links to each experience are not available, this project would

probably be used as a website to promote a new AR game.
